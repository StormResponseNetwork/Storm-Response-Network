Our Mission

Awareness before impact. Support after the storm.

Weather Awareness

Clear public information based on official forecasts, warnings, radar, and verified observations.

Storm Spotting

Responsible ground-truth reporting of hail, wind, flooding, damage, and dangerous storm conditions.

Emergency Communications

Amateur-radio readiness, disciplined net procedures, and resilient information flow.

Disaster Support

Preparedness, volunteer coordination, relief supplies, and community assistance after severe weather.

Leadership

Founder-led with nationwide goals.

SRN is currently led by Graham Butler, Founder and Executive Director, with a mission to grow a professional volunteer network that serves communities nationwide.
