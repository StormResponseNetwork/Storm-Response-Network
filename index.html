Mission Capabilities

One network. Multiple ways to protect communities.

SRN brings together public weather information, responsible observations, emergency communications, training, preparedness, and disaster-support goals.

01

Weather Intelligence

Official forecasts, warnings, radar, severe-weather outlooks, and local forecast search.

02

Ground Reports

Clear, concise reports of hail, wind, flooding, damage, and storm structure.

03

Communications

Amateur-radio readiness, net procedures, backup power, and message discipline.

04

Preparedness

Training paths, emergency kits, warning reception, first aid, and household planning.

05

Situation Reporting

Structured public briefings with timestamps, areas, threats, and operational posture.

06

Disaster Support

Transparent fundraising goals for approved preparedness and community-support needs.
