Large Hail

Report size using a ruler or common object comparison. Avoid collecting hail during dangerous lightning.

Damaging Wind

Report measured speed when possible, plus downed trees, power lines, roofs, or structures.

Flooding

Report location, depth estimate, moving water, road impacts, and trapped people. Never enter floodwater.

Tornado / Funnel

Describe condensation, debris, rotation, location, and movement. Do not exaggerate uncertain features.

Damage

Identify what is damaged and the approximate area. Do not enter unstable structures or restricted scenes.

Emergency

Call 911 for immediate life-safety threats. Discord is not emergency dispatch.
