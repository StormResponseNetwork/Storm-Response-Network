Volunteer With SRN

Bring your skills to the network.

SRN is building a nationwide volunteer community focused on weather, communications, preparedness, public information, and disaster support.

Weather Spotter

Observe and report severe weather using clear, accurate, and safe methods.

Amateur Radio

Support communications, nets, exercises, and radio-readiness education.

Public Information

Create safety graphics, updates, educational posts, and public resources.

Training Support

Help organize legitimate training resources and member development.

Disaster Support

Assist with supplies, outreach, cleanup support, and community needs when appropriate.

Discord Staff

Help moderate channels, organize information, and maintain a professional community.

Application Process

Applications are handled through Discord.

Join the server and contact SRN leadership. Applicants may be asked about availability, experience, certifications, interests, age, and preferred role.

Apply Through Discord
Volunteer expectations
  • Follow safety and conduct rules
  • Never claim authority not granted
  • Respect public-safety instructions
  • Protect private information
  • Complete role-specific training
  • Represent SRN professionally
