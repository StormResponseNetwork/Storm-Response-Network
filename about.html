About SRN

Mission, purpose, and leadership.

Storm Response Network is a nationwide independent community network focused on weather awareness, reporting, preparedness, communications, and disaster support.

Mission Statement

Protect communities before, during, and after severe weather.

Storm Response Network exists to protect communities through severe-weather awareness, responsible storm spotting and reporting, emergency communications, preparedness education, and organized disaster-relief support. SRN works to help people make informed decisions before storms, communicate critical observations during hazardous weather, and support recovery efforts after disaster strikes.

Core Values

Safety

People come before reports, media, vehicles, equipment, or content.

Accuracy

Confirmed facts are separated from estimates, interpretation, and speculation.

Service

We exist to help communities, not to seek authority or attention.

Integrity

SRN communicates transparently and never claims affiliations or powers it does not have.
